<span class="reviewsection">Premise:</span> Our guy has been working at the same paper company for about twenty five years, but then one day he gets fired after he opposes some downsizing. He tries and tries to find another job for over a year with very little luck... until he realizes that there's an easy solution to his problem: if there are no other candidates, he will get the job. Now, what would a good, *permanent* way to get rid of those other candidates...?<br/>

<span class="reviewsection">Under 90 minutes?</span> Nah, it's a korean movie, it's over two hours long<br/>
<span class="reviewsection">Do they say the title?</span> Oh yes they do! And what is more: they also *imply* it many times which pleases me greatly.

<span class="reviewsection">First thing I thought of:</span>

![delightfully devilish](no-other-choice.gif)<br/>
<span class="reviewsection">Okay, more:</span> Delightfully devilish. Really really funny black comedy, I love how all characters are dumb as hell and at no point they seem to make any good decisions. Really cool stuff, I had a blast!<br/>

<span class="reviewsection">A simple way to improve it:</span> There was a fart scene in the movie. This is not a complaint, if anything I wish there was a callback to that scene, later.
